Why Martinique?

Martinique’s Vibrant Heritage

Martinique is a stunning Caribbean island known for its rich culture, beautiful beaches, and vibrant history. With its mix of French and Creole influences, it offers a unique blend of European charm and tropical allure.

Its diverse landscape, from volcanic mountains to crystal-clear waters, makes it a perfect destination for both adventure and relaxation.

Beaches

My favourite beaches in Martinique

Le Diamant

With its golden sands, turquoise waters, and the striking Diamond Rock, Le Diamant in Martinique is a serene paradise. The town is ideal for peaceful strolls, swimming, or soaking in stunning sunsets. It’s a haven where nature’s beauty and history blend seamlessly to create a memorable experience.

Coordinates:

Latitude: 14.480023
Longitude: -61.033310

What I like about it

Le Diamant has a special energy that keeps me coming back. Standing before Diamond Rock, you can feel the weight of its history, while the Cap 110 Memorial offers a moving reminder of the past. The town’s vibrant markets and delicious Creole cuisine add to its charm, making it a perfect mix of history, culture, and natural beauty.

Pointe Faula

With its shallow turquoise lagoon, soft golden sands, and steady trade winds, Pointe Faula in Martinique is a favorite for watersports enthusiasts and beach lovers alike. From kitesurfing to paddleboarding, or simply relaxing on the shore, it’s a place that invites both adventure and calm.

Coordinates:

Latitude: 14.545375
Longitude: -60.839890

What I like about it

Pointe Faula stands out for its peaceful vibe and stunning shallow lagoon, perfect for watersports or just wading through the calm waters. The steady breeze and serene atmosphere make it a favorite spot to relax or enjoy outdoor activities, offering the best of both worlds.

Sainte-Marie

Sainte-Marie, located on Martinique’s northeast coast, combines breathtaking beaches with a dramatic, rugged shoreline. Its tranquil stretches of sand and lush surroundings make it a peaceful escape for anyone seeking natural beauty and relaxation. It’s a perfect blend of calm and stunning scenery.

Coordinates:

Latitude: 14.781371
Longitude: -60.993063

What I like about it

Sainte-Marie’s beaches have a raw, untamed charm that I love. The golden sands, framed by lush green hills, create a peaceful retreat that feels untouched. Whether strolling along the shore or taking in the ocean views, it’s a place that lets you truly unwind and connect with nature.
